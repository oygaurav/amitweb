FAQ

To open a support ticket, send an email to support@cliqnget.com
Include any relevant information about your query in your email, an agent from the support team will be in touch within 8 hours.

To withdraw your purchased rewards, email our Support: support@cliqnget.com
Make sure you include your website username & region within the email. Our Representatives will aim to get back to you within 8 hours and deliver your redeemable Activation Code for the reward that you have purchased from our website.
